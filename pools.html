IT IS TIME TO SWIM

OUR POOLS

THE ROUNED POOL

For our youngest swimmers and families, we offer the circled pool, designed with safety and fun in mind. The shallow waters and gentle entry make it the ideal spot for children to gain confidence in the water. Our colorful play features, like fountains and water toys, ensure endless entertainment for the little ones. Parents can rest easy knowing their kids are in a secure environment, where certified lifeguards keep a watchful eye.

LARGE POOL

Dive into the heart of aquatic bliss in our spacious large pool. Whether you're a serious swimmer or just looking to cool off and have some fun, this is the place for you. With crystal-clear water and a comfortable, inviting atmosphere, our large pool is perfect for lap swimming, water aerobics, or simply basking in the sun on the poolside. Our lifeguards are always on duty to ensure your safety, so you can relax and enjoy your swim to the fullest.

SLIDERS

Looking for an adrenaline rush? Take the plunge down our thrilling water slides! Our slides are a favorite among visitors of all ages. Zip down the twisting turns, feel the excitement as you race to the bottom, and make a big splash! These slides provide an exhilarating experience while maintaining the highest safety standards. If you're seeking an adventure in the water, our slides are the ultimate attraction.

WATER GAMES

Are you a fan of friendly competition? Join in the action with our water basketball games. Grab a ball and challenge your friends or fellow swimmers to a game of hoops in the pool. It's a fantastic way to stay active and cool off at the same time. Our poolside hoops and equipment are waiting for you, so come and show off your skills or learn the basics of water basketball.
